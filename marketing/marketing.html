Search

Engine Optimization

Search Engine Optimization is one of the fastest ways to grow your business in an increasingly competitive market.

A strong SEO strategy is the best way to appear at the top of the search engine results page. The more search visibility you have, the more qualified traffic you can send to your website. At Big Drop, we work to connect business, brands and partners with the people who are interested in them.

Search engine optimization techniques, when done right, exponentially increase your Google page visibility and in turn greatly improves your web traffic. And web traffic is exactly why your site exists. With more people checking out your site, the more people you get who are buying what you’re selling. Those first-time buyers ultimately become dedicated customers who promote your awesome services or product to their circle of family and friends. The ROI impact that SEO has on your business is pretty revolutionary!

Media Marketing

We’re good at reaching users who are likely to become customers. We target ads based on demographics such as age, gender, language, location, interests, and online shopping habits. We can also remarket to users who have already shown interest. There we will find related audiences who are similar to your existing customer base.

SMM is all about intent, so whether your target audience is looking to make a purchase or learn more about your business, showing them the right ad, at the right time will bring qualified traffic to your website and increase the opportunity for conversions.

Cross-promotion is one of the most effective ways to boost your following. We Post your content on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and other platforms. It encourages users to follow you on any and all Platforms. We use these and different strategies to drive traffic to the platform where we get the most engagement rate from your target audience.

eMail

Marketing

Email Marketing is consistently among the highest ROI’s of all of the marketing channels. Unlike other email marketing companies, Appify is a unique email marketing agency that uses a combination of beautiful email marketing newsletter design with a cohesive email marketing solutions.
